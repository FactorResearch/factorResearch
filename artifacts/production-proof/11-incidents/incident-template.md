## Timeline

Record UTC timestamp, actor, observation/action, correlation or evidence reference, and result. Never paste secrets, tokens, customer financial records, or unnecessary personal data.

## Decisions

Record decision, alternatives, owner, approver, time, and rollback condition.

## Containment and Recovery

Record traffic/feature controls, credential actions, evidence preservation, data validation, synthetic results, SLO observation window, and closure approval.

## Corrective Actions

Every action requires an owner, due date, severity, verification method, and tracking link. Repeated findings block release until the systemic cause is removed.
