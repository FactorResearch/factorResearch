# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026

This Policy applies to subscriptions purchased directly from Cenvarn. Purchases through an app store or third-party marketplace may be governed by that provider’s rules.

## Cancellation

You may cancel a subscription through the billing settings or by contacting [support@cenvarn.com].

Unless otherwise stated at checkout:

- Cancellation stops the next automatic renewal.
- Paid access continues until the end of the current billing period.
- Deleting the application or stopping use does not cancel billing.
- Account deletion and subscription cancellation are separate actions.

## Refunds

Before launch, choose and publish one of these models:

### Recommended launch rule

Payments are generally non-refundable after a billing period begins, except:

- Where required by applicable law.
- Duplicate or clearly erroneous charges.
- A documented failure that prevented meaningful use of the paid Service for a substantial portion of the billing period.
- A refund expressly promised in a promotion or plan description.

### Optional customer-friendly rule

First-time subscribers may request a refund within **[7 or 14 days]** of the initial purchase if usage remains below **[defined threshold]**. Renewals are non-refundable unless required by law.

Do not promise both models. Remove the unused option before publication.

## Annual plans

State whether annual plans are:

- Refundable on a prorated basis;
- Refundable only during an initial period; or
- Non-refundable after purchase except where law requires otherwise.

## Trials

Trial length, conversion date, renewal price, and cancellation deadline will be disclosed before enrollment. Cancelling before the trial ends prevents conversion to a paid plan.

## Price changes

Price changes apply to a future renewal after required notice. Users may cancel before the new price takes effect.

## How to request a refund

Email [support@cenvarn.com] with:

- Account email.
- Transaction date.
- Transaction identifier.
- Reason for the request.

Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method. Processing times depend on the payment provider.

Nothing in this Policy limits non-waivable consumer rights.
