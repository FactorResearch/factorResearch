# Accessibility Statement

Cenvarn is committed to making its website and Services usable by people with disabilities.

## Our goals

We aim to:

- Support keyboard navigation.
- Provide visible focus states.
- Maintain readable contrast.
- Use semantic structure and labels.
- Provide text alternatives for meaningful images.
- Avoid relying on colour alone to communicate scores, risk, warnings, or status.
- Make charts and tables understandable through labels, summaries, and accessible data.
- Support zoom, responsive layouts, and common assistive technologies.
- Provide accessible error, loading, and retry states.

We use the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA as our target where reasonably applicable.

## Known limitations

Before publication, list actual known limitations and planned fixes. Do not claim full compliance unless it has been tested.

## Feedback

Report an accessibility barrier at [support@cenvarn.com]. Include the page, feature, assistive technology, browser, and a description of the problem where possible.

We will acknowledge feedback and work toward a reasonable response or alternative format.
